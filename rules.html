-=[ Server Rules ]=-

Please follow these guidelines to keep our community awesome!

~*~ Rule #1: Age Requirement ~*~

You must be 13+ to join this server (duh). This is both Discord's policy and our server rule.

~*~ Rule #2: Be Respectful ~*~

Light swearing is allowed but don't be mean to anyone. Treat everyone with respect - no harassment or bullying.

~*~ Rule #3: No Spam ~*~

Please don't spam messages, emotes, or mentions. Keep the chat clean and readable.

~*~ Rule #4: No Advertising ~*~

Don't advertise other servers, social media, or products without permission.

~*~ Rule #5: Partnership Requests ~*~

If you want to partner with us, please DM an admin or make a ticket. Don't ask in public channels.

~*~ Rule #6: Don't Be Annoying ~*~

Respect others' experience. Excessive trolling, mic spamming, or disruptive behavior isn't cool.

~*~ Rule #7: No Flooding ~*~

Don't flood channels with GIFs, images, or long text. Keep your media sharing reasonable.
